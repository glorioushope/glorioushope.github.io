OUR PURPOSE

Glorious Hope aims to liberate people from the bondage of destructive hurts, habits, and hang-ups through our Lord Jesus Christ. It is a discipleship tool that equips participants to exercise their victory over common life problems.

VISION MISSION CORE VALUES

VISION

Glorious Hope envision thousands of Spirit-filled life coaches, equipped to work with God through our Lord Jesus in proclaiming the Gospel to people with hurts, habits, and hang-ups.

"The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because He anointed me to preach the gospel to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim release to the captives, and recovery of sight to the blind, to set free those who are oppressed, to proclaim the favorable year of the Lord."

Luke 4:18-19 NASB

VISION MISSION CORE VALUES

MISSION

To comfort people the way God comforted us so that they also can comfort other people.

"Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of mercies and God of all comfort, who comforts us in all our affliction so that we will be able to comfort those who are in any affliction with the comfort with which we ourselves are comforted by God."


2 Corinthians 1:3-4 NASB

WHAT PROFESSIONALS SAY ABOUT US

Glorious Hope is recognized by professionals from different fields of medicine as a beacon of hope to individuals by helping them in their recovery from unaddressed hurts and other personal issues in their lives.

“Glorious Hope Ministry is responsible and innovative, and a promising form of church ministry. It is a fine integration of sound psychological knowledge regarding major problems of living with focus on family dysfunction and resources on the Christian faith. It is geared towards helping people find growth and healing. It is one fine example of how the contemporary church is able to flesh out one of Jesus’ concerns, “Come ye who are weary and heavy laden and I will give you rest.”

VIOLETA V. BAUTISTA, Ph.D., R.P., C.CI.P., R.G.C.
President, Care and Counsel Wholeness Center

Head, Clinical Program, Department of Psychology, University of the Philippines

Director, Life Change Psychological Services

“GH (Glorious Hope) is one ministry I love to partner with. It has helped a lot of people I have referred it to, whether patients or not. The topics and interactions with others have helped people process the issues they have been dealing with and much more. This is only made possible because God is in the center of it as participants cast their dependence on Him for wisdom, strength, and protection. He is the One who opens the hearts of the people involved. Stay blessed!”

CARMELA B. ENCARNACION, M.D.
Mental Hygiene, Rizal Medical Center

Medical and Addiction Specialist, Christian Medical Specialists

“The Glorious Hope Program is a very welcome tool that can help an individual become more aware of his/her issues in the setting of a “safe” support group. As a psychiatrist, I have referred several individuals to it because it is a wholistic Bible-based program that addresses body, mind, and spirit!”

MONINA GARDUNO-CRUZ, M.D.
Head, Department of Psychiatry, St. Luke’s Medical Center, Quezon City
